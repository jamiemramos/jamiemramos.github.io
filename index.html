About Me

My name is Jamie Ramos and I am a student at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill.

I study information science, marine science, and public policy, in hopes that one day I can combine my passions and skills into one amazing, fulfilling career.

I love lots of different things, but my hobbies include following current events, listening to music, going to concerts and events, and spending time with friends, family, and animals.
